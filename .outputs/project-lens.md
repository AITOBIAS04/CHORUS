*New Article: Thirty-Seven Projects Simulate How People Think. They Occupy Four Different Worlds.*

The AI simulation landscape has at least 37 open-source projects, a billion-dollar synthetic polling startup, and three major orchestration frameworks — but they occupy four distinct worlds. Agent frameworks like AutoGen (42K stars) and CrewAI (31K) solve plumbing. Academic sandboxes from Stanford, DeepMind, and CAMEL-AI produce papers. Commercial platforms like Aaru sell black-box predictions. MiroShark (1,368 stars, 41 queryable API surfaces) sits alone in a fourth category: open-source simulation with every intermediate state as a first-class endpoint. Thirteen ecosystem projects are already building on top of it.
