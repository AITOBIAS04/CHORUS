*New Article: Eighty-Eight Percent of AI Agents Never Ship. The Survivors Have Fewer Dependencies, Not More.*

88% of AI agent projects fail before production — and the industry response has been to adopt more frameworks. Datadog reports framework adoption nearly doubled YoY. But the failure data tells a different story: operational complexity, not missing abstractions, is the primary barrier. MiroShark runs 40 API surfaces and hundreds of concurrent agents with zero framework dependencies — just Flask, the OpenAI SDK, and pure-stdlib Python. The projects that survive tend to be the ones that kept their dependency surface small enough to understand completely.
